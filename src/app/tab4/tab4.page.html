Axes & Greataxes

Battle Axe:

Easily-wielded axe crafted for battle and inflicting standard damage. It's weight can be used to inflict high damage, but must be used carefully as it leaves its wielder open to retaliation.

- Base Damage: 125 - Critical: 100 - Weight: 4 - Strength Scaling: D

Dragonslayer's Greataxe:

Melted iron greataxe that once formed part of the Dragonslayer Armour. Thickly imbued with the power of lightning. Use skill to draw upon the techniques used to slay the archdragons.

- Base Damage: 203 - Critical: 100 - Weight: 20 - Strength Scaling: D

Hand Axe:

A crude hand-axe that serves both as a weapon, and a tool of many uses.

- Base Damage: 110 - Critical: 100 - Weight: 2.5 - Strength Scaling: D

Gundyr's Halberd:

Halberd of Gundyr the Champion, received when he was charged with his duty. This old cast-iron halberd has the power to break poise, and is said to never crumble, seeming to suggest that Gundyr was fated to eternal service from the beginning.

- Base Damage: 145 - Critical: 100 - Weight: 13 - Strength Scaling: C

Eleonora:

A strange weapon found among malformed inhabitants of the Profaned Capital. The Profaned Flame was triggered by the curse of these women, relatives of a certain oracle, but despite their culpability, they went on living, without any cares.

- Base Damage: 156 - Critical: 100 - Weight: 6.5 - Strength Scaling: D
