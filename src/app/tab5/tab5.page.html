Bows & Greatbows

Black bow of Pharis:

A black longbow named after a hero of old, known for the unusual stance from which it is fired.

- Base Damage: 67 - Critical: 100 - Weight: 3 - Dexterity Scaling: C

Composite Bow:

Composite short bow emphasizing power. Its size allows for quick shots. Though more powerful than standard bows, it also requires more strength of the user, and its range is short.

- Base Damage: 73 - Critical: 100 - Weight: 3.5 - Strength Scaling: D

Darkmoon Longbow:

Longbow of Darkmoon Gwyndolin, who was gradually devoured by Aldrich. This golden bow is imbued with powerful magic and is most impressive with Moonlight Arrows.

- Base Damage: 36 - Critical: 100 - Weight: 2.5 - Dexterity Scaling: D

Dragonslayer's Greatbow:

reatbow used by the Dragonslayers during the age of gods. Far greater in size than any normal bow, and far more devastating.

- Base Damage: 145 - Critical: 100 - Weight: 13 - Strength Scaling: C

Sniper's Crossbow:

Long-range heavy crossbow used by Carim snipers. Its lengthy base makes aiming difficult, and accurate usage requires a trained, dexterous hand.

- Base Damage: 70 - Critical: 100 - Weight: 7.5 - Strength Scaling: No Scaling
